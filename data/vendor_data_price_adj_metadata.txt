Database of each unique listing of online pets and associated date and price

Field descriptions

1. col_sp: species name, referenced to Catalogue of Life (http://www.catalogueoflife.org/col/)
2. price: the price of the pet in the listing in US$ (not adjusted for inflation)
3. wbm_timestamp: the date that the web archive (http://web.archive.org/) archived a given listing
4. name of vendor:
	1. bw: Backwater Reptiles, http://www.backwaterreptiles.com/
	2. ll: LLL Reptiles, http://www.lllreptile.com/
	3. ur: Underground Reptiles, https://undergroundreptiles.com/
5. vendor_id: unique listing ID generated by authors
6. count: number of duplicate listings
7. year: year of listing, calculated from 'wbm_timestamp'
8. pai: price adjusted for inflation to December 2016 US$
